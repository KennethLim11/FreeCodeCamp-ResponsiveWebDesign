Lebron James

Timeline

1984

Lebron Raymone James was born on 30 December 1984 in Akron, Ohio (USA).

2003

On the drafts of 2003 he was picked by his hometown team, the Cleveland Cavaliers.
LeBron sings a seven-year shoe contract with Nike. The contract was worth around $90 millions.

2004

Lebron becomes NBA rookie of the year.

2007

LeBron played his first NBA final in 2007. His team, the Cavaliers lost.

2009

In the 2009 he won the MVP NBA award. It is an award that is given to the best performing player of the season.

2010

For the second time in a row, Lebron was chosen as the NBA MVP.

2012

Miami Heat wins the NBA championship finals. This was Lebron's first championship and his third appearance in the finals of the NBA Championship.

2014

Lebron returns to the Cleveland Cavaliers.

2015

Nike signs a lifetime deal with Lebron.

2016

Cleveland Cavaliers came back from a 3-1 series to defeat the Golden State Warriors in the NBA championship finals.

2017

Lebron officially scored more points in the playoffs than Michael Jordan. Jordan held the record for two decades.

2018

Lebron joined the Lakers.

2020

Lebron wins his fourth NBA championship and is Finals MVP that year.
